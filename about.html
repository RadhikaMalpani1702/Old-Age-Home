About Us

Housai Old Age Home was founded with a heartfelt mission, to create a space where elderly individuals are not only cared for but truly valued and respected. We believe that growing older should never mean growing lonely. That’s why our home is built around the principles of community, dignity, and warmth. We provide a secure and supportive environment that fosters meaningful connections, promotes independence, and ensures holistic well-being for all our residents.

From daily healthcare support to cultural activities and personalized attention, everything at Housai is designed to enrich the lives of our senior residents. Our dedicated team of caregivers, medical professionals, and volunteers work around the clock to create a homely, compassionate atmosphere. At Housai, every resident is seen as a cherished member of our extended family, where memories are honored, comfort is a priority, and every day is celebrated with love and purpose.
